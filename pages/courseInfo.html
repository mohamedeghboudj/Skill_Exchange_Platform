Course Details

Course nameWeb Development

TeacherDr. Carter


Price$49


Duration6h 30m

Master the fundamentals of modern web development and learn to build dynamic, responsive websites from scratch. This course covers everything from HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to advanced front-end frameworks and back-end integration. You’ll design visually appealing interfaces, connect them to databases, and deploy your projects online. Whether you’re a complete beginner or looking to level up your skills, this hands-on program will guide you through real-world projects, best practices, and the latest tools used by professionals in the industry.

Instructor

Dr.Mahmoudi

Dr. Mahmoudi Mohammed is a passionate web development instructor with over 8 years of experience in front-end and back-end technologies. he specializes in modern JavaScript frameworks, responsive design, and UI/UX best practices. Mahmoudi has mentored hundreds of students to launch successful tech careers. His teaching style blends theory with real-world project experience. He believes in empowering learners to build innovative and user-centered digital solutions.
